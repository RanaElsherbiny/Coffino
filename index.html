WELCOME TO Coffino's Events

We source our beans from sustainable farms and roast them in small batches for unmatched freshness. Every sip tells a story of passion, precision, and purpose.
Stay tuned for upcoming events — because at Coffee Haven, there’s always something brewing!

From Bean to Brew – Coffee Journey Experience

Ever wondered what makes your coffee taste so special? Join us for an immersive session where we take you behind the scenes — from the origin of our beans to the final pour in your cup.

Our head roaster will walk you through the coffee life cycle, share roasting secrets, and let you sample different roasts to explore how flavor evolves.

  • Date: First Sunday of every month
  • Time: 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM
  • Entry: Free with RSVP

Latte Art Throwdown – The Barista Challenge

It’s time for some friendly foam competition! Watch skilled baristas from around the city go head-to-head in a latte art showdown — hearts, tulips, swans, and more.

Guests can cheer, taste, and even vote for their favorite design. Whether you’re a coffee lover or just here for the art, it’s an event you won’t forget.

  • Date: Last Saturday of every month
  • Time: 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM
  • Entry: Free for guests | Barista registration required
